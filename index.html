Courses Offered

GIT offers various degree courses in Engineering at graduate level(B.E).

Chemical Engineering

The Department of Chemical Engineering at GIT was started in 2007. Today the department is recognized as a leading department in GIT primarily because of its strong academic programme. The Department is equipped with 12 labs, departmental computer centre, departmental library, seminar hall, classrooms with modern teaching aids and staff rooms.

Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering is one of the largest departments in the Institute, with 10 full-time faculty membes and 5 full-time administrative and technical support staff. The Department is equipped with 16 labs, departmental computer centre, departmental library, seminar hall, classrooms with modern teaching aids and staff rooms.

Computer Engineering

Students aspiring for engineering admission always prefer GIT's Computer Engineering Department.The Department is equipped with 8 labs, 148 No. of latest generation Computers, departmental computer centre, departmental library, seminar hall, classrooms with modern teaching aids,staff rooms and WiFi connectivity.
